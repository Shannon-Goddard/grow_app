What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (e.g., phone, computer) when you visit our website or use our apps. They help us understand how the Service is used without identifying you personally.

Cookies We Use

We use Google Analytics cookies to collect non-personalized data, such as:

  • General location (e.g., country, city)
  • Device type (e.g., phone, computer)
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Browser type and operating system

These cookies are:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary, deleted when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: Stay on your device (up to 14 months) to track usage over time.

In the future, growappcannabis.guide may use advertising cookies if we add ads (the app and games won’t have ads). We’ll update this policy if that happens.

Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Improve the Service (e.g., fix bugs, optimize features).
  • Analyze usage trends (e.g., which pages are popular).
  • Plan for future ads on growappcannabis.guide.

Your Choices

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect some Service features (e.g., Analytics-driven improvements). To opt out of Google Analytics, use their opt-out tool. For future ad cookies, we’ll provide opt-out options if implemented.

The Service is for users 21 and older for cannabis cultivation purposes. Cookies don’t collect data about your cannabis use, only general usage stats. You’re responsible for complying with local, state, and federal laws (cannabis is illegal under U.S. federal law).
