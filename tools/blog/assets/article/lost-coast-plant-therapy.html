How to Use Lost Coast Plant Therapy Like a Pro

Wanna crush pests and keep your grow room pristine? Here’s my routine, plus tips to maximize Lost Coast’s magic:

  • Foliar Spray: Mix 1 oz per gallon of water, spray leaves top and bottom during veg or early flower. Do it at lights-out to avoid burn.
  • Soil Drench: Use 1-2 oz per gallon and pour into soil every 2 weeks to keep root pests at bay.
  • Grow Room Deep Clean: Mist fabric pots and surfaces with a 1 oz/gallon mix. Pair with 50/50 distilled water and 3% peroxide for walls and gear.
  • Clones & Seedlings: Lightly mist with a 0.5 oz/gallon mix to protect young plants without stress.
  • Pro Tip: Test on one plant first to dial in your mix. Always shake the bottle well—those oils need to blend! [1]
