How to Pick Your LED

Choosing an LED’s like picking the right strain—know your vibe:

  • Small tents (2x2): 200-300W, like Spider Farmer SF-2000.
  • Bigger grows (4x4): 400-600W, try Mars Hydro TSW-2000.
  • Loud buds: Look for UV/IR diodes to boost that aroma.
  • Light intensity: Aim for 700-1000 µmol/m²/s in flower—think big buds! [2]
