Why Nutrients Are Everything

Think of your plants as hungry kids. Without the right snacks, they stay scrawny and sad. Nutrients are like a buffet of nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium—NPK for short. Nitrogen grows green leaves, phosphorus builds strong roots, and potassium pumps up fat buds. Skimp out, and you’re stuck with airy nugs that don’t hit. [1]

I’ve seen it—too much or too little, and your plants throw a tantrum. Balance is key, and MyGrow nutrient schedule keeps it chill. [2]
