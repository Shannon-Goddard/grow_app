GrowTogether: Spotlight on Your Tools

GrowTogether is now all about celebrating the cannabis community’s creativity! Got an app, blog, YouTube channel, or other tool you built to help growers? We want to feature it! How to Get Featured

Ready to shine? Here’s how to get your tool featured:

  • Share your creation: Send us details about your app, blog, YouTube channel, or other grow tool.
  • Tell your story: Why did you create it? What problem does it solve for growers?
  • Include links: Drop links to your tool, website, or channel so we can check it out.
  • Add visuals: Got screenshots, photos, or videos? Send ‘em to show off your work.
  • Email us: Fire everything over to growapp@loyal9.app.

We’ll feature the dopest submissions in the GrowTogether section, both on the app (for current users) and our website. Let’s inspire the cannabis community with your ingenuity!
